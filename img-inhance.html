Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vazzle free to use?

Yes, Vazzle is completely free to use. We support our service through ads that you can optionally watch to support us and get faster processing.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

What is the maximum image size I can compress?

You can compress images up to 20MB in size. For best results with very large images, we recommend compressing them in smaller batches.

Why should I compress my images?

Compressed images load faster on websites and apps, improve user experience, reduce bandwidth usage, and can improve your SEO rankings.
